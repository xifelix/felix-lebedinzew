Felix Lebedinzew
Digital Product Designer

Hi, my name is Felix and I´m a digital product designer born and raised in Berlin. It is my passion to solve problems with a human-centered approach and create – from the initial idea to the final release.

Currently I work for Neugelb Studios GmbH – a subsidiary of the Commerzbank. Next to that I am seeking for M.Sc. in Entrepreneurial Design Management at Loughbourough University in London, UK.

In the past years I did some freelance work, founded my own design studio ⟶ PixelPink and build and sold a printing startup called DeinWunschmotiv.de

I specialize in the Conception and Visual Design of mostly mobile Apps, Websites and digital Products. My approach is always striving for a minimalistic and effective user-centered solution combining function and beauty. Last but not least, I´m not a fulltime developer yet always extending my coding skills in frontend and React, being able to build the bridge between design & business & development.
